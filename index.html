How can Crowdtalk find the target group I define?

Crowdtalk has an automated algorythm that uses google ads and social media to find the people you defined as a target group. This makes your job far easier, all you need to do is telling us who you are looking for.

Why would customers fill out the forms created with Crowdtalk?

Customers are rewarded with a certain amount of money you define. This is the incentive that will motivate them to answer the questions. The amount you defined as a prize appears automaticaly on the account of the customers.

How will I see the result of a campaign?

After a customer filled out a form you have created, your dashboard is updated with the new data, and the reports you requested get updated right away.

Reach your customers where they are

Crowdtalk can be accessed both on desktop and mobile. This allows you to better reach your customers and makes it more comfortable for them to answer your questions.
